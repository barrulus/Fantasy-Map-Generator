FMG GeoJSON Export Bookmarklets

Export Fantasy Map Generator data as standard GeoJSON files

How to Use

  1. Drag any blue bookmarklet button to your browser's bookmark bar
  2. Open Fantasy Map Generator and generate or load a map
  3. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmark bar to export
  4. The GeoJSON file will download automatically

Alternatively, click Copy to copy the script, then paste it into the browser console (F12 > Console) on the FMG page.

    GeoJSON files use WGS84 (EPSG:4326) coordinates and can be opened in geojson.io, QGIS, or any GIS software.
